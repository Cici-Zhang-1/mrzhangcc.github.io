I will participate the test again

2018-02-23 散文·生活 生活·美国

Today, I will participate a test on 3-Hour Alcohol and Drug Education Test of Maryland again. This is for getting driver license of Maryland. I said again because I had done this about one and half year ago, but the certificate was expired few month ago. You may want to know why this happened? Because I have one year duration to get my driver license. Yes, you are right, I have procrastination.

In my last year』s plan list, I had made a decision to pass the test, but I failed to finish it. I always found a excuse to not do it all the time. Now I have to re-do what I have did before. Such behavior has brought me huge losses.

1. Time

As I have got my driver license when I was in China. Now in order to be able to drive here. I have to spend time on researching how to to it and what the whole flow is. Then I followed the steps. First, I have to study online at least 3 hours on Alcohol and Drug Education and then pass the related test. Then I need to study traffic laws in order to pass the law test. When I participated the law test, I went three times to the MVA. It really took me a long time.

2. Money

When I first took the exam, I almost spend two hundred dollars on it which includes test fee and transportation fee. As we did not have a car, every time I went to the MVA to take the knowledge test, I have to take Uber or Lyft.

3. Vigor

Each time you have to study online. So you have to spend vigor on it. Otherwise, you can not pass the test.

4. Opportunity

At the begin of this year, I decided to leave the restaurant which I have worked for one year and to find another job which is related my major. I was very glad I found one soon. But there is a problem, the company is too far from my home. As the public transportation is not as mature as China, I can not arrive there by the public transportation. I did not have a car. I even did not have the driver license. Then how can I find the next job without a car?

I have never calculated gains or losses on my procrastination. But today, when I am thinking carefully, I am very shocked. In China, there is a saying: You Always Give Up Halfway. I do not want to be such a man.
